## view information of PARTITIONS with MySQL

```sh
time mysql -uroot -Nse "
SELECT TABLE_SCHEMA
     , TABLE_NAME
     , PARTITION_NAME
     , PARTITION_ORDINAL_POSITION
     , TABLE_ROWS
  FROM INFORMATION_SCHEMA.PARTITIONS
"
```

## GRANT with MySQL

```sh

GRANT ALL PRIVILEGES ON `tbl`.* TO 'user'@'%'

REVOKE ALL ON `tbl`.* FROM 'user'@'%';

FLUSH PRIVILEGES;

```
